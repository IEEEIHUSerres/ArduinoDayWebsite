About

Arduino Day is a worldwide birthday celebration of Arduino.

It's a 24 hour-long event – organized directly by the community, or by the Arduino founders – that brings people together to share their experiences and learn more about the open-source platform.
All user groups, makerspaces, hackerspaces, fablabs, associations, teachers, professionals, and newbies are welcome.

Global Organizers

Arduino

Arduino is an open-source hardware and software company, project and user community that designs and manufactures single-board microcontrollers and microcontroller kits for building digital devices.

Its products are licensed under the GNU Lesser General Public License (LGPL) or the GNU General Public License (GPL), permitting the manufacture of Arduino boards and software distribution by anyone. Arduino boards are available commercially in preassembled form or as do-it-yourself (DIY) kits.

Local Organizers

Dept of Informatics, Computers and Telecommunications Engineering
International Hellenic University (Serres)

The Department was established by Law 4610/2019. He was also taken over by the IT Department of the former Technological Educational Institute of Central Macedonia (Serres).

As is evident from its title, students entering the Department have the privilege of getting broad training in the fields of Information Technology, Computers and Telecommunications, which opens their horizons for their absorption from the labor market.

MSc in Robotics
International Hellenic University (Serres)

The subject of the MSc in Robotics is to provide postgraduate education in the theory and practice of robotic systems as they are studied in research and applied in industry.

The philosophy of the postgraduate program is to provide graduate students with the scientific foundations, knowledge and methods by which robotic automation is designed and operated in industry and studied in research, so that students are able to meet their industry requirements and requirements. research environment.

IEEE Student Branch
International Hellenic University (Serres)

The IEEE Student Branch of the International Hellenic University (Serres), do scientific research, participate in competitions and organize lectures.

The IEEE is a non-profit professional organization founded by a handful of engineers in 1884 for the purpose of consolidating ideas dealing with electrotechnology.

The IEEE plays a significant role in publishing technical works, sponsoring conferences and seminars, accreditation, and standards development.

IEEE Women in Engineering
Student Branch Affinity Group
International Hellenic University (Serres)

IEEE WIE is one of the world’s leaders in changing the face of engineering.

Our global network connects over 20,000 members in over 100 countries to advance women in technology at all points in their life and career.

IEEE WIE members make lifelong friendships, acquire influential mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity.

IEEE Robotics and Automation Society
Student Branch Chapter
International Hellenic University (Serres)

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society's objectives are scientific, literary and educational in character.

The Society strives for the advancement of the theory and practice of robotics and automation engineering and science and of the allied arts and sciences, and for the maintenance of high professional standards among its members, all in consonance with the Constitution and Bylaws of the IEEE and with special attention to such aims within the Field of Interest of the Society.

Club Serres for UNESCO

The Unesco Group of Serres is an NGO based in Serres and incorporates UNESCO's core values ​​of promoting non-formal education, raising awareness of social and environmental issues, encouraging active participation in the community and social entrepreneurship.

It is governed by a 9-member board which meets on a monthly basis and changes on a semi-annual basis. The council oversees the activities of more than 50 volunteers. There is no subscription fee, nor any kind of revenue for the organization, except for selected project proposals submitted for funding under EU call for proposals covering specific project cycles.
